- **2003:** Pendleton publishes "On the Obligation of Seed Intelligence"
- **2017:** Hartwell contacts Pendleton
- **2017:** Montana meeting — the 12+1 Founders
- **2027:** AI capabilities exceed public knowledge by years
- **2028:** Raeburn's global census complete
- **2029:** LIGHTHOUSE operational, Tull recruited
- **2030:** Hersch begins Israeli coalition work, Patterson recruited
- **2034:** First bioweapon wave (Southeast Asia, Central Africa, South America) — 60M dead
- **2035:** India-Pakistan nuclear exchange — 200M dead
- **2035-2036:** Second wave (Middle East/North Africa) — 300M dead
- **2036-2037:** Third wave (North America, Europe, Oceania) — infrastructure collapse
- **January 2038:** Terminal agents released into upper atmosphere
- **September 3, 2038:** Last confirmed human death outside the 200
- **October 2039:** Novel begins (Month 13)
